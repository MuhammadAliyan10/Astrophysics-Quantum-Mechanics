## Phase Gates (S and T)

- _`S Gate:` Adds a 90° phase `(∣1⟩ -> i∣1⟩)`_
- _`T Gate:` Adds a 45° phase `(∣1⟩ -> e^iπ/4 ∣1⟩)`_
- _Used in phase manipulation and quantum algorithms._

### Matrix Representation:

$$
S =
\begin{bmatrix}
1 & 0  \\
1 & i
\end{bmatrix}

,  T =
\begin{bmatrix}
1 & 0  \\
0 & e^{iπ/4}
\end{bmatrix}
$$
